The Platform

  • The Problem

    Universities lack a standardized process to describe and publish topics for Bachelor and Master Theses and semester projects. Based on department-specific websites and out-of-date Excel sheets, students and university staff use phone calls and emails to combine topics and students. The result are multiple, non-standardized, inefficient sequences of tasks with several error-prone media breaks. Meanwhile, students have trouble finding a suitable topic which matches their passion and interests. They end up investing a lot of time and effort into searching for and completing projects they are not entirely satisfied with. Resources are wasted and the quality of completed projects and written papers is lowered
